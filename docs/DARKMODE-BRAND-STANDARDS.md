# DARKMODE Brand Standards — Foundation 0.1

## Brand architecture

**DARKMODE** is the parent brand.

**DARKMODE STORIES** is the formal product name. In normal interface language and conversation, use **Stories**.

## Core visual principle

Darkness creates focus. Color behaves as light.

The interface should feel cinematic, premium, dimensional, and playful without becoming visually noisy.

## Color system

- Void Black: `#020204`
- Panel Black: `#0A0D14`
- Electric Cyan: `#00EAFF`
- Signal Purple: `#7C5CFF`
- Hot Pink: `#FF3DBB`
- Energy Orange: `#FF8C42`
- Light Yellow: `#FFE45C`
- Electric Lime: `#8CFF54`
- Primary White: `#F7F8FF`
- Muted Text: `#97A0B5`

Use gradients primarily for borders, active states, product titles, dividers, and motion accents. Avoid flooding large reading areas with saturated color.

## Typography

- Product and studio titles: extremely bold, compact, high-impact sans serif.
- Utility labels: smaller, widely tracked uppercase.
- Body and child-facing text: simple, highly legible sans serif.
- Avoid decorative fonts in functional interfaces.

## Shape

- Rounded panels: 24–30 px radius.
- Utility buttons: 15–20 px radius.
- Primary microphone control: circular.
- Borders should feel illuminated rather than merely outlined.

## Motion

- Motion should communicate state, reward action, or create depth.
- Button presses compress before returning.
- Active controls glow rather than changing to unrelated colors.
- Parallax layers move at different rates, with the nearest layer moving fastest.
- Keep ambient motion slow and subtle.
- Respect future reduced-motion accessibility settings.

## Glow hierarchy

1. Active primary control
2. Selected tool or mode
3. Panel border
4. Ambient background

Not every object should glow equally.

## Interaction language

- One tap performs one obvious action.
- One drag has one meaning at a time.
- Large touch targets are mandatory.
- The center microphone is the primary action.
- Never require a child to understand professional creative software terminology.

## Original-content rule

Do not ship third-party cartoon characters, logos, branded creatures, copied sticker packs, or identifiable franchise artwork without a license.

Use:
- Original Darkmode artwork
- User-owned family artwork/photos with permission
- Unicode emoji rendered by the operating system
- Properly licensed assets with documented rights
